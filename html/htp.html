How to play

Welcome to Mob Raids!!

Rules are simple: choose a gamemode you want to play and press 'Start'. Each gamemode has its own unique rules.

Basic gameplay rules:
- Use 'A' and 'D' buttons to move left and right
- Press 'Space' to shoot arrows at the enemies

Standard:
This is the standard, 1 minute long game mode. All you have to do here is score as many points as possible in one minute. Be careful though... As the time passes by, 'Phantoms' get more aggressive!

Wave survival:
In this gamemode, you are in 'The Nether' fighting 'Ghasts'. Survive as many waves as possible.
