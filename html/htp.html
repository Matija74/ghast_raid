How to play

Welcome to Ghast Raid!

Your goal is to survive waves of enemies and score as many points as you can.
- Use 'A' and 'D' keys to move.
- Press 'SPACE' to shoot.
- Each ghast you hit gives you 10 points.
- For every missed shot you lose 2 points.
- Every ghast that makes it past you takes away 5 points.

Good luck, warrior!
